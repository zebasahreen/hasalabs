About the App

Better Deen is built on the productivity principle of habit streaks — the idea that you should mark off each day you complete a task and work to build the longest continuous streak possible. Applied to salah, this means logging all five prayers every day and challenging yourself never to break your streak.

The app is entirely free and contains no advertisements, because your prayer time deserves to be distraction-free.

Key Features

🕐

5 Daily Prayers

Track Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha individually every day.

🔥

Habit Streaks

Build and maintain prayer streaks. See your longest streak and current streak at a glance.

📅

Calendar View

View your prayer history in a monthly calendar to spot patterns and gaps.

🚫

Zero Ads

No advertising. Ever. Your worship is too important for interruptions.
