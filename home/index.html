Make Salah a Habit

Better Deen is built on a simple but powerful idea: track each prayer, build streaks, and challenge yourself never to break them. It's the same productivity approach used by top performers — applied to the most important act of worship.


In Sha Allah, with consistency and intention, this app can help you become someone who never misses a prayer.
