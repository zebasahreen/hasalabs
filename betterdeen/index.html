Better Deen

Build and maintain the three pillars of a fulfilling Islamic life: Worship, Knowledge, and Service — one day at a time.

Download on Google Play

Three Pillars of a Fulfilling Islamic Life

Better Deen brings together everything you need to grow spiritually — track your prayers and Quran reading, deepen your knowledge of Islam, and build a habit of giving and good deeds. All in one clean, distraction-free app. Free, no ads, no account.

Worship

🕐

Smart Prayer Logging

Track your five daily Salah with time-aware logging — prayers can only be marked after their time arrives.

🔥

Streaks & Heatmap

View individual prayer streaks and a monthly heatmap calendar to see your consistency at a glance.

📖

Quran Reading Log

Log your daily Quran reading time and build a consistent habit of engaging with the Book of Allah.

📍

Accurate Prayer Times

Prayer times calculated automatically from your device location using your preferred calculation method.

🔔

Adhan Notifications

Set personalised notifications for each prayer — choose off, a standard beep, or the full Adhan sound.

Knowledge

🕋

Juz Amma Memorization

Track your memorization progress surah by surah. Read the full Arabic text with transliteration and English translation, verse by verse.

🎧

Audio Recitation

Listen to complete surahs or individual verses. Choose from multiple reciters and enable repeat mode for revision.

📜

Daily Seerah

Read a new entry from the Prophet's biography every day, based on Ar-Raheeq Al-Makhtum. Build a daily reading streak.

🤲

Daily Duas

Read authentic duas for morning, evening, sleep, and more — with Arabic text and transliteration.

Service

✅

Good Deeds Log

Log a good deed every day from a curated list or write your own. Build a lasting habit of kindness.

💝

Charity Tracker

Record your charity giving and add personal reflections. Build a regular habit of giving.

Progress

📊

Unified Dashboard

View your weekly and monthly progress across all three pillars in one dashboard. Stay consistent, stay motivated.

🚫

No Ads. No Account. No Tracking.

Completely free with zero advertisements. No account required. All your data stays privately on your device, never sent to any server.
