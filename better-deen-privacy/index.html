Privacy Policy for Better Deen

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Data Collection

Better Deen does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data (prayer logs, habits, progress) is stored locally on your device only.

Location

Your device location is used solely to calculate accurate prayer times. Location data is never stored or transmitted.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Better Deen does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: developer.hasalabs@gmail.com
